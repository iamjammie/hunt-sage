title: "OSINT Flight"
excerpt: "Track an aircraft, locate a safe house, and recover the final flag using open-source intelligence."
order: 1
---

A journalist disappears mid-flight. Your mission:

1. **Extract GPS metadata** from a provided `skyline.png` image.  
2. Use open tools to identify the **nearest airport**.  
3. Track a **historical flight** that matches time and location.  
4. Infer the **safe house city** where the journalist is likely taken.

Flag format:

```text
flag{ICAO_code_city}
```
